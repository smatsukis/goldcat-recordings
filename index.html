Our Services

We are specialists in the following areas.
Don't see what you looking for? Contact us with your request and we will assist you - we do it all!

AUDIO RECORDING, MIXING & MASTERING

Live concerts, studio recordings, music exams, conferences, and special events. Let us take care of all things audio - from the recording, to top tier in-studio mixing, and mastering to ensure your levels meet all streaming platforms standards and your project is clean and professional.

PODCASTS

Take your podcast or audiobook to the next level with a team of dedicated mixing and editing engineers. We will edit and clean up your interviews and ensure all streaming and distribution platform standards are not only met, but exceeded.

RADIO ADVERTISEMENTS

Let us take your brand to the next level with a unique jingle, voice over, and of course only the best audio quality for you radio advertisement. We have in house composers that will work with you to create something truly special for your company.

VIDEO

Flexible options available including single cam, or multi-cam operators to ensure the highest quality video work to match your audio – ready and edited for your distribution and social media needs.

ALBUMS

Using the finest audio equipment, let us record, mix and master your next album. With 6 Albums and 2 awards under our belt (Including a billboard number 1), we have you covered from start to finish.

VOICE OVERS

Narrate your audio book, advertise your small business, or get your brand out there on social media – let us help you with any and all of your voice over needs.
